Trading Strategy Guide

Rule Bot Strategy

Buy Strategy

  • MA5 above MA20
  • Price above MA60 trend line
  • Volume > 1.2x 60-period average
  • MACD histogram positive and increasing
  • RSI(21) below 85 to avoid overbought

Sell Strategy

  • ATR-based dynamic stop loss on entry
  • Stop = price - 2×ATR, min 90% of entry
  • Trailing stop activates after 15% profit
  • Stop moves to 3% below new high

Trading Rules

  • Open position only when all buy conditions met
  • Max ~1/3 cash per trade
  • No re-entry while holding
  • Sell all when stop loss or trailing stop triggers

ML Bot Strategy

Reinforcement Learning

  • Based on Q-learning algorithm
  • Learn optimal policy from historical data
  • Continuously optimize decisions via reward mechanism
  • Balance exploration and exploitation (ε-greedy)

State Features

  • Current price and price change rate
  • RSI and MACD technical indicators
  • Position status (cash/stock)
  • Asset change rate

Trading Rules

  • Max 50% of available cash per trade (up to 30 shares)
  • Can execute buy, sell, or hold actions
  • Transaction costs affect reward calculation
  • Exploration rate decays over time
